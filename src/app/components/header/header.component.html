Test Instructions

Please carefully follow the steps outlined below. Once you complete a step, you will not be allowed to go back. Ensure that you have a stable internet connection, and make sure your camera is enabled for the duration of the test.

Step 1: Instructions

Read all the instructions provided for the test. You are required to follow them strictly throughout the process.

Step 2: Candidate Biography

In this step, you are required to provide your personal details including your full name, upload your CV, and fill out some biography-related questions. Ensure that your CV is in PDF format and contains all the relevant details about your skills, projects, and previous experience. Double-check this information before proceeding as you cannot edit it later.

Step 3: HR Round

During the HR round, you will be presented with several multiple-choice questions. Each question will have four options, and you are required to select both the most effective and least effective option for each situation or scenario. Carefully read through the options and make your selection based on what you think is the best approach.

Step 4: Technical Round

In the technical round, you will interact with our chat bot. The bot will ask questions based on your skills, projects, and experience as mentioned in your CV. The questions may vary depending on your area of expertise, so be prepared to explain and discuss your technical background in detail.

Step 5: Video Submission

For the final step, you are required to record a 3-minute video where you answer a question posed by our team. Make sure your camera is turned on and that your responses are clear and concise. This video will serve as a final assessment of your communication skills and how you present yourself.

Important Rules:

  • No malpractice or cheating is allowed during the test.
  • Your camera will be turned on for the entire duration of the test to monitor you.
  • You cannot switch tabs or navigate away from the test screen. Any such activity will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Once you complete a step, you will not be able to return to the previous step.
  • The test will only be considered complete after the video submission in Step 5.
